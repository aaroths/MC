My Checkup is new way for you to support yourself. We all have normal ups and downs, but sometimes we get stuck in a bad spot. My Checkup gives you a way out before you get too stuck.

My Checkup is about finding things you Do or Think or Feel when you are well. Of course we aren't going to feel well all the time, but for each of us, there are certain things that keep us on the right track.

Check-In allows you to see how you are doing.

It can also keep track of stats and correlate your statements with how well you feel. We recommend you check-in weekly but you can check in as frequently or rarely as you want.

Why participate?

My Checkup is a commitment to getting to know yourself and to finding the person you enjoy being. This tool is currently being developed, and can promise no studied outcomes. By participating, we hope you will give us feedback make this tool as useful as possible and to study its uses. We believe My Checkup can act to improve your understanding of yourself and also to warn you when your mindset is changing.

Resilient Studio

My Checkup is a product made by Resilient Studio. The goal of Resilient Studio is to create helpful tools to improve wellness and to help overcome the many challenges in life.
