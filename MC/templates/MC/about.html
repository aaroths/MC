My CheckUp is new way for you to support yourself. We all have normal ups and downs, but sometimes we get stuck in a bad spot. MyCheckup gives you a way out and an early warning sign before you get too stuck.

Why participate?

My CheckUp is a commitment to getting to know yourself and to finding the person you enjoy being. This tool is currently being developped, and can promise no studied outcomes. By participating, we hope you will give us feedback make this tool as useful as possible and to study its uses. We believe My Checkup can act to improve your understanding of yourself and also to warn you when your mindset is changing.

Resilient Studio

My CheckUp is a product made by Resilient Studio. The goal of Resilient Studio is to create helpful tools to improve wellness and to help overcome the many challenges in life.
