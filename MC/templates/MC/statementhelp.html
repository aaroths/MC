Statements are things that you Do or Think or Feel when you are well.

You can think of them as goals to perform. However, they are often small things that don't require major changes. To perform these statements, it often involves us paying a bit more attention.


One way to think of statements:

  • Recall a time in your life in the past few years you were feeling well
  • Were there certain activities that you were doing?
  • Were there certain thoughts you were having?
  • Were there certain feelings that you were having?

A statement should:

  • Start with "I ..." or a verb "eg: Doing, Feeling, Being, Trying, Going etc"
  • May be the cause of you feeling well
  • Or may be something you do because you feel well
