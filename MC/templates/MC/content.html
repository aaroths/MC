My CheckUp is new way for you to support yourself. We all have normal ups and downs, but sometimes we get stuck in a bad spot. MyCheckup gives you a way out and an early warning sign before you get too stuck.

Your own My CheckUp

Consider this a work in progress. You will work on creating a list of 5 statements. When you feel well and equilibrated, there are certain interests, thoughts or feelings you tend to have. Try to capture these in your statements. When you are in a "well" state of mind, these statements usually apply to you.

This list will be revised with time. Not only will it warn you when things are getting tough, but it will also bring awareness to the things that keep you balanced.

Why participate?

My CheckUp is a commitment to getting to know yourself and to finding the person you enjoy being. This tool is currently being developped, and can promise no studied outcomes.
