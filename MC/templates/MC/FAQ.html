Why participate?

My Checkup is a commitment to getting to know yourself and to finding the person you enjoy being. This tool is currently being developed, and can promise no studied outcomes. By participating, we hope you will give us feedback make this tool as useful as possible and to study its uses. We believe My Checkup can act to improve your understanding of yourself and also to warn you when your mindset is changing.
